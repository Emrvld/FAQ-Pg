Frequently asked questions:

How to contact customer support? Click on the need help section in the Customor support page.
How to recover email & password? Click on the forgot password link at the right corner under the field you entert password.
Where do I find warranty information on products? In the section view more products, scroll to the bottom and click the option view warranty for our products
How to delete my account? Click onto My account then select the option Manage account, click suspend or remove account.
How to change my payment option/ remove & add new card Go to account, Click Managae account, select Payment options, select add or remove card.
